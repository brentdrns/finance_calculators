About VDOT

VDOT is a measure of your current running fitness developed by Dr. Jack Daniels. It reflects your effective VO2max — not just your raw aerobic capacity, but your ability to use oxygen efficiently at race effort.

Higher VDOT = faster equivalent performances. Training zones are calculated as percentages of your VDOT pace to elicit the right physiological adaptation.

For informational and training purposes only. Training paces derived from Jack Daniels' VDOT system. Individual response to training varies. Consult a qualified running coach before beginning a structured training program.
