How US Wealth Is Distributed
These bars show what share of total US wealth each group holds — not how many people are in each group. Every group contains exactly 10% of households, but their share of national wealth is wildly unequal.

Percentile estimates are based on population-level survey data and involve interpolation between anchor points. Individual results are approximate. Income data: U.S. Census Bureau CPS ASEC 2024 (reporting 2023 income). Wealth data: Federal Reserve Survey of Consumer Finances 2022. This is not financial advice.
