Why Abel built this

The standard mortgage refinance calculator asks for your rate and loan balance, then tells you your new monthly payment. That's not a decision — that's arithmetic.

A real decision requires modeling what happens to your equity over time, what you give up by not investing the closing costs, how your tax deduction changes, whether PMI drops off, and exactly how many months until you break even. That's what our calculators do. Abel oversaw the entire development from the couch and is satisfied with the results.
